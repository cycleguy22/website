Editing and proofreading services

Our service

We offer a friendly and professional service to people who need help with the editing or proofreading of technical documents. We accept documents from all fields, but specialise in science and technical subjects; our core subjects are physics, engineering and materials science.

We can help with spelling, punctuation and grammar in addition to references and formatting according to the style guides of peer-reviewed journals. We will also read your document to check the overall flow of the text, as well as consistency in use of terminology, numbering of equations, use of acronyms and so on.

We encourage those for whom English is a second language to get in touch; we know the idiosyncrasies of science writing can be problematic even for native speakers! Scientific research can generate highly complex documents due to the presence of equations, diagrams and highly specialised terminology - as a result even the most assiduous writer may be affected by errors. You can greatly reduce the chances of mistakes affecting your document by employing a fresh set of eyes.

Our philosophy at Chiltern Academic is 'communication matters'. Our mission is to help clients communicate their work in a clear, professional and error-free manner.

Documents we work on

Drafts of scientific journal articles, theses, essays, white papers and more.

Subject areas

We can proofread documents in any field, but specialise in physics, engineering and materials science.

Pricing

Our standard rate is £24 per thousand words for a basic proofreading of a document. The rate for more extensive editing is £30 per thousand words.
